German Wheellock Gun 1632 Code: PAR001

<%= require("fs").readFileSync(htmlWebpackPlugin.options.filePath + '/compartir.html') %>

Description

Faithful replica of a German wheel gun from the year 1632 used in the famous 30 years war.

It works with iron pyrite to generate the spark as the original and is and is equipped with a key to assemble the mechanism, turning the wheel tightens a flat spring in the form of "V" transmitting the movement by a chain of steel links.

Features

Enfield Pistol (Barnett)
Barrel: steel, exterior, conical, smooth interior (as its original)
Caliber: .58
Lock: Percussion
Carriage: walnut
Guards: polished brass
Ramrod: poling
Weight: approx. 1350 gr.
