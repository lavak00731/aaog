Description

This is a beautiful model of a Japanese Fuse Pistol. It came about in response to many people who asked for a model suitable for competition in Tanzutsu category.

Of fine lines and classic construction, it has a unique grip that allows the shooter to aim in a relaxed manner. Its caliber is identical to that used by other models accepted in this category. It is smooth bore and the aiming devices are matched to the original.

Inspired by the shape of a Samurai pistol found in Kumamoto Castle, Japan, handmade with the best walnut, magnificent polished brass and the best steels, to offer the greatest delight when practicing this exciting sport.

In the published video, you can see the original pistol with a red background, located in the Kumamoto museum in Japan, and on a white background our replica. Thanks to a computer effect, the two images are transparent to compare the quality of the reproduction.
