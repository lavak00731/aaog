Harper´s Ferry Pistol Code: PC004

Description

Replica of the famous Harper's Ferry military pistol. It is considered the first military weapon manufactured in the American territory and was used to secure the independence declared by the northern country a few decades ago.

Built between 1805 and 1807, it is a symbiosis between English and French guns, with the appearance of a new American style. The flintlock is sturdy enough to ignite smoothly with every shot.

Its smooth barrel 58 caliber gives a singular power to the shot, being a pistol that stands out for its elaborate ramrod pass. It is made of wood and has a bronze finish to facilitate loading. Accompanying the pommel of the gun are two spurs that are inserted into the wood to serve as reinforcement. It is considered one of the most beautiful military pistols ever built.
