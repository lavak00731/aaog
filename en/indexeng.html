Welcome to Armas Antiguas Osvaldo Gatto

We are a small Italian family dynasty of traditional gunsmith located in Argentina, offering the best standards and quality control for our products, under the strictest international manufacturing methods. It is our goal to guarante safety, durability and fun to all our selected clients.

All the pieces, you are going to watch in this site, are fully handmade. All these items are muzzleloading firearms completely functional, they are capable of shooting lead ammunition using black powder. We invite you to sail our site and meet our wide range of products.

Products

We have a wide range of products.

Pistols and Rifles

Long, one-handed muzzleloaders, functional replicas (they shoot with black powder) of weapons which had forged History.

Go to Pistols and Rifles

Artillery

We manufacture a wide range of field and naval cannons, also mortars, faithful and accurate replicas of weapons used by fleets and armies of great empires. All our artillery is capable of shooting lead ammunition using black powder.

Go to Artillery

Accesories

We put to your disposition a large variety of indispensable implements for the muzzle-loading shooting. Powder horns, charging ramrods, calepinos and all the ammunition caliber molds used by the weapons we make.

Go to Accesories
